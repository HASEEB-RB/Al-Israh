Our Story

Born in Lahore, Built for Pakistan

Al-Israh Paint is a proudly Pakistani brand based in Lahore — a city famous for its craftsmanship and industrial excellence. We set out with a simple mission: to give Pakistani homes the quality of paint they deserve at a price that's fair.

Over the years, we have expanded our product range to cover every painting need — from interior emulsions and exterior weather shields to wood varnishes, primers, and specialty coatings. Our Gold Series brings international-grade colour cards and formulations to the local market.

Today, Al-Israh is trusted by homeowners, builders, and contractors across Punjab and beyond. Our tagline says it all — Color Your Life.
